About

I'm a robotics PhD with industry experience in software engineering and diverse autonomy applications. With a strong background in machine learning, computer vision, and AI for real-time motion control systems, I bring versatility and expertise to the field of robotics.

After completing my PhD at Cornell University, I joined Moog's Technology and Advanced Pursuits group in 2020. Here, I have been instrumental in developing innovative autonomous solutions across sectors such as space, defense, and onstruction. My contributions span from perception and navigation algorithm development to diverse applications like autonomous construction equipment, reinforcement learning-controlled excavator arms, and defense industry multi-object tracking.

Prior to joining Moog, I served as a Research Assistant at Cornell's Laboratory for Intelligent Systems and Controls (LISC). During my time there, I devised novel methods and algorithms by merging real-time computer vision with automatic control theory. I focused on intelligent scene perception and control solutions for the Office of Naval Research (ONR), targeting resource-constrained mobile platforms.

Throughout my career, I have excelled in leading cross-functional teams, conducting independent research, and effectively communicating complex concepts to diverse audiences. My results-driven approach has led to securing substantial research funding and showcasing my expertise at flagship conferences and publications. As a confident technology communicator, I am dedicated to making complex scientific concepts accessible and fostering trust among peers, collaborators, and clients.

I bring a wealth of experience from my roles as a Systems Engineer at Moog Inc., a Graduate Research Assistant and Teaching Assistant at Cornell University, and an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the State University of New York at Buffalo. My educational journey includes a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Mechanical Engineering and a Master of Science (MS) in Mechanical Engineering from Cornell University, as well as a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering from the University at Buffalo.

I'm thrilled to share my knowledge, experiences, and ongoing projects through this website. Join me as we delve into the fascinating world of robotics, AI, and autonomous systems, and discover the endless possibilities of cutting-edge technology!
